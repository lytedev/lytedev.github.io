You should override the "main" block in your site's layouts/index.html to change this content: 

{{"{{"}} define "main" {{"}}"}}
  <p>This will be your site's homepage's new content!</p>
{{"{{"}} end {{"}}"}}

You can change the title as well by overriding the "title" block: 

{{"{{"}} define "title" {{"}}"}}
  Index - {{ .Site.Title }}
{{"{{"}} end {{"}}"}}
